hello,
I am currently the Co-Director of Design at Other People. Together, with a team of core staff, visual artists, and web-developers, we are aiming to create to inspire creative expression on students from all disciplines through the inception of UC San Diego's first literary magazine.

I was previously a visual designer at AS Graphic Studio. Through this experience, I have had the oppurtunity to work with such clients as Vince Staples, Hailey Kiyoko, Stephanie Soo, and Popeye the Foodie Dog. I am also studying literature/writing at UC San Diego.

Much of my work strives to be human-centered and locally-focused, designed to bring out the best of what surrounds it.
